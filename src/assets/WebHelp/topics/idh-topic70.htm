The extension worker, whenever a new farmer is assigned to him/her, registers the farmer. This is different from creating an account which is the work of the administrator, like a new stake holder or a new extension worker etc.

 

Some of the things extension workers do:

•Check and register which crop type the farmer actually planted

•Whenever a new farmer is assigned to him/her, registers the farmer.

•The extension worker will get daily updates on whether information of that specific environment.
